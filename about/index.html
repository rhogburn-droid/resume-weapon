Why We Built This

Roughly 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS software before a human ever reads them — often for reasons that have nothing to do with the candidate's qualifications. A two-column layout, a missing keyword, or the wrong file format can be enough to get a perfectly qualified person auto-rejected.

We built Resume Weapon to level the playing field. Instead of paying $50/month for an ATS tool or guessing what's wrong, anyone can upload their resume, get an instant compatibility score, see exactly which keywords and formatting issues are hurting them, and generate an optimized version — for free.

Who Runs Resume Weapon

Resume Weapon is built and operated by a small, independent team with backgrounds in hiring, recruiting, and machine learning. We've sat on both sides of the hiring process — reviewing resumes as hiring managers and building the parsing systems that score them. That combination is what informs how our checker evaluates resumes.

We are not a large corporation or a venture-backed startup chasing your data. We're a focused tool that does one thing well: help you get past the bots and land interviews.

How We Make Money

The ATS score, keyword analysis, and AI-generated resume preview are completely free, with no signup and no usage limits. We earn revenue only when someone chooses to download a clean, watermark-free version of their optimized resume for $2.99, or subscribes to unlimited downloads for $9.99/month. That's it. We don't sell data, run ads, or charge hidden fees.

Your Privacy Matters

Because you're uploading a resume with personal information, we take privacy seriously. Resume parsing happens in your browser. We don't sell your data, we don't use your resume to train AI models, and we don't share your information with third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
