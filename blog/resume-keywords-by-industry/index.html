Top ATS Resume Keywords by Industry

Updated March 2026 · 10 min read

Different industries use different language, and ATS systems scan for industry-specific terms. Using generic keywords when your target role has specific terminology is one of the fastest ways to get a low ATS score. Here are the most important keywords by industry, drawn from analyzing thousands of job postings.

Technology & Software

Tech roles have the most keyword-dense job postings. ATS systems in tech are aggressive about filtering for specific tools and methodologies.

Languages & Frameworks: Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Node.js, Java, Go, Rust, SQL, GraphQL, REST API, microservices

Cloud & Infrastructure: AWS, Azure, GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, Terraform, Linux, serverless

Methodologies: Agile, Scrum, Kanban, DevOps, test-driven development, code review, sprint planning

Tools: Git, GitHub, Jira, Confluence, Datadog, Splunk, Jenkins, CircleCI

Healthcare

Healthcare ATS is heavily compliance-focused. Missing certifications or regulatory keywords will immediately filter you out.

Compliance: HIPAA, Joint Commission, FDA, CMS, OSHA, quality assurance, regulatory compliance, patient safety

Clinical: patient care, EMR/EHR, Epic, Cerner, clinical documentation, care coordination, patient outcomes

Certifications: RN, BSN, CNA, ACLS, BLS, CPR, NP, PA-C, board certified

Operations: care delivery, population health, value-based care, telehealth, patient satisfaction, readmission reduction

Finance & Accounting

Finance roles emphasize regulatory knowledge, analytical tools, and specific certifications.

Skills: financial modeling, forecasting, budgeting, variance analysis, P&L management, revenue recognition, GAAP, IFRS

Tools: Bloomberg, SAP, Oracle, QuickBooks, Excel (advanced), Power BI, Tableau, SQL

Certifications: CPA, CFA, CFP, Series 7, Series 66, FRM, CAIA

Regulatory: SOX compliance, SEC reporting, audit, risk management, internal controls, due diligence

Sales

Sales ATS filters heavily on methodology knowledge, CRM experience, and metric-driven language.

Metrics: quota attainment, ARR, MRR, pipeline generation, deal size, win rate, revenue growth, customer acquisition

Methodologies: MEDDIC, MEDDPICC, Challenger Sale, SPIN, Solution Selling, consultative selling, value selling

Tools: Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Gong, ZoomInfo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Clari

Skills: enterprise sales, B2B, SaaS, account management, territory planning, negotiation, executive engagement, channel partnerships

Marketing

Marketing roles blend creative and analytical keywords. ATS in marketing scans for both channel expertise and measurement capability.

Channels: SEO, SEM, PPC, content marketing, email marketing, social media, influencer marketing, ABM, demand generation

Tools: Google Analytics, Google Ads, HubSpot, Marketo, Mailchimp, Hootsuite, SEMrush, Ahrefs, Figma

Metrics: ROI, ROAS, conversion rate, CAC, LTV, click-through rate, engagement rate, marketing qualified leads (MQL)

Skills: brand strategy, content strategy, go-to-market, product marketing, competitive analysis, A/B testing, marketing automation

Engineering & Manufacturing

Engineering ATS is certification and methodology-heavy, with specific software tools being critical keywords.

Tools: AutoCAD, SolidWorks, MATLAB, LabVIEW, PLC programming, CAD/CAM, FEA, CFD

Methodologies: Six Sigma, Lean Manufacturing, Kaizen, 5S, FMEA, root cause analysis, design of experiments

Certifications: PE, PMP, Six Sigma Black Belt, Green Belt, OSHA 30, AWS Certified Welding Inspector

Skills: process optimization, quality control, supply chain, GMP, ISO 9001, project management, technical documentation

How to Use These Keywords

Don't just copy-paste keyword lists into your resume — ATS systems and recruiters will both flag this. Instead, weave relevant keywords naturally into your experience descriptions and achievements. The best approach is to compare these industry keywords against the specific job posting you're targeting.
