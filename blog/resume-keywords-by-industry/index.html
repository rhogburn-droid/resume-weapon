Using the wrong industry vocabulary is one of the most common reasons qualified candidates get filtered out by applicant tracking systems.

Below are the most important ATS keywords for every major industry, organized by category. Use these as a starting point — but always prioritize the specific terms from the job posting you're applying to.

Technology & Software Engineering

Programming: Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, Go, Rust, C++, Ruby, SQL, HTML/CSS, React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Django, Flask, Spring Boot.

Cloud & Infrastructure: AWS, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Azure, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, CI/CD, microservices, serverless, infrastructure as code.

Data & AI: machine learning, data pipeline, data engineering, ETL, Spark, Hadoop, TensorFlow, PyTorch, natural language processing, computer vision, SQL, NoSQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB.

Methodology: Agile, Scrum, Kanban, DevOps, SRE, test-driven development, code review, sprint planning, JIRA, Confluence.

Healthcare

Compliance: HIPAA, FERPA, Joint Commission, CMS regulations, clinical compliance, patient privacy, regulatory compliance, accreditation.

Clinical: patient outcomes, evidence-based practice, clinical documentation, EHR (Electronic Health Records), Epic, Cerner, MEDITECH, patient care, care coordination.

Certifications: RN, BSN, MSN, NP, PA-C, CNA, ACLS, BLS, CPR, board certified.

Finance & Accounting

Technical: financial modeling, financial analysis, GAAP, IFRS, SOX compliance, Basel III, risk management, portfolio management, derivatives, fixed income, equity research.

Tools: Bloomberg Terminal, SAP, Oracle Financials, QuickBooks, Excel (advanced), VBA, Power BI, Tableau.

Certifications: CPA, CFA, CFP, Series 7, Series 63, Series 66, FINRA, FRM.

Sales & Business Development

Strategy: pipeline management, revenue growth, account management, territory development, consultative selling, solution selling, enterprise sales, SaaS sales, B2B, B2C.

Metrics: quota attainment, conversion rate, customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), monthly recurring revenue (MRR), annual recurring revenue (ARR), win rate.

Tools: Salesforce, HubSpot, Gong, Outreach, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo, CRM.

Marketing

Digital: SEO, SEM, PPC, Google Ads, Meta Ads, content marketing, email marketing, marketing automation, conversion rate optimization, A/B testing, demand generation.

Analytics: Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, attribution modeling, funnel analysis, customer segmentation, data-driven marketing.

Tools: HubSpot, Marketo, Mailchimp, Hootsuite, Sprout Social, Canva, WordPress, Webflow.

Engineering & Manufacturing

Technical: CAD, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, CATIA, MATLAB, finite element analysis, lean manufacturing, Six Sigma, quality assurance, process improvement, GMP.

Certifications: PE (Professional Engineer), Six Sigma Green Belt/Black Belt, PMP, OSHA.

Human Resources

Functions: talent acquisition, employee relations, benefits administration, performance management, compensation, HRIS, onboarding, workforce planning, succession planning, DEI (Diversity, Equity, Inclusion).

Tools: Workday, ADP, BambooHR, Greenhouse, Lever, iCIMS, UltiPro, Paylocity.

Compliance: FMLA, ADA, EEOC, Title VII, FLSA, workers compensation, labor relations.

How to Use These Keywords

These lists are starting points, not checklists to copy-paste. The most effective approach is to read the specific job posting, identify which of these terms appear, and then incorporate those exact terms into your resume. A resume that matches 80%+ of the job posting's keywords will score well on virtually any ATS.

Remember: multi-word phrases like "pipeline management" or "patient outcomes" carry more weight than single words. Always use the complete phrase as it appears in the posting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What keywords should I put on my resume?
Match the specific terms from the job posting — hard skills, tools, certifications, and industry terminology. Every industry has different high-value keywords.
What are the best keywords for a tech resume?
Programming languages (Python, JavaScript), frameworks (React, Node.js), cloud (AWS, Azure), and methodologies (Agile, CI/CD). Always match the specific stack in the posting.
How do I find industry-specific keywords?
Read 5-10 postings for your target role and note recurring terms. Use Resume Weapon to compare your resume against any posting automatically.
Do keywords differ by industry?
Significantly. Healthcare needs HIPAA and EHR. Finance needs GAAP and SOX. Marketing needs SEO and conversion rate. Generic keywords lower your ATS score.
