Talking Points for Advisors

  • Roughly 75% of resumes are filtered by ATS before a human reads them — often for formatting, not qualifications.
  • A "designed" two-column template can score far worse than a plain single-column one, because parsers scramble multi-column layouts.
  • Tailoring keywords to each posting is the highest-impact move — and the tool shows exactly which keywords to add.
  • Students should aim for an 80+ score before submitting.

Yes. No licensing fees, no per-seat costs, no contracts. Career centers simply link students to the free tool — unlimited use, no signup required.

No. Nothing to install, integrate, or provision. Unlike licensed platforms, it's just a link you add to your resources page or LMS.

Licensed tools require annual contracts, per-student pricing, and IT integration. Resume Weapon is free with no licensing or procurement — a no-cost supplement or alternative with unlimited access.

Yes. No seat limits or accounts means current students and alumni alike get the same free ATS scoring and AI optimization.
