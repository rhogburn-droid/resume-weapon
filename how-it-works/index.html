How Scoring Works

When you upload your resume, we extract the text exactly as an ATS would. If you paste a job posting, we compare your resume against that specific role's keywords and requirements — this gives the most accurate score. Without a posting, we score against general ATS formatting and structure best practices.

The result is a 0-100 compatibility score. Above 80 means you'll pass most ATS filters. Below 70 means you're likely being auto-rejected. You also see which specific keywords are missing and which formatting issues need fixing.
